About Us

Our Mission

HANK Services connects teenagers with seniors in our community, creating meaningful relationships while providing valuable assistance with everyday tasks.

We believe that both generations have much to offer each other: teens gain valuable work experience and the satisfaction of helping others, while seniors receive needed assistance and the joy of mentoring young people.

Benefits

  • Affordable help for seniors
  • Valuable work experience for teens
  • Building intergenerational relationships
  • Creating stronger community bonds
  • Supervised, safe environment for all
